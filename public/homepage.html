Online job portal is the primary mediator between the job seeker and job provider.
In present time, there is hardly any job portal which focuses on providing jobs to the individuals who have low skills or education not upto a certain level.
Our main objective is to help the individuals to seek jobs requiring low skills and simultaneously allowing both individuals and organisations to post jobs.
Unlike other similar job portals we allow the following job types:
1. Carpenter
2. Security Guard.
3. Tutor
4. Housemaids.
5. Driver.
6. Janitor.
and many more.....
